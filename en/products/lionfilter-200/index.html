Lionfilter 200

Lionfilter 200
LIONIC DPI · Next-Gen Firewall

10G High-Speed NGFW with AI Protection, VPN & LTE Failover

LionFilter 200 is LIONIC's flagship next-generation firewall, featuring 1×10G WAN and 4×2.5G LAN high-speed ports with 4 Gbps protection throughput. Integrates cloud AI zero-day virus detection, IPSec Site-to-Site VPN, WireGuard VPN, LTE failover, HA high availability, web activity management, and intelligent QoS bandwidth scheduling.

Core Features

Lionfilter 200 Filtering Capabilities

AI Zero-Day Protection

Cloud AI engine identifies zero-day viruses and dynamic malicious URLs, comprehensively blocking malware and botnet attacks.

Malicious Site Blocking

Real-time interception of phishing, C2 command-and-control, and malware distribution domains, preventing employees from accidentally visiting malicious sites.

HTTPS Deep Filtering

SSL decryption content inspection penetrates encrypted traffic for URL classification and content filtering — no encrypted blind spots.

IPSec / WireGuard VPN

Supports IPSec Site-to-Site VPN for connecting branch office networks, and WireGuard Client-to-Site VPN for remote employee access.

LTE Failover

Automatically switches to an LTE USB dongle or mobile hotspot when WAN is interrupted, then switches back automatically when restored — ensuring uninterrupted connectivity.

HA High Availability

Supports HA high availability configuration with two LionFilter 200 units — backup device automatically takes over on failure, ensuring continuous network connectivity.
